Packaging

Each piece ships in a small rigid box with tissue paper and a ribbon. Inside you will find the piece itself, a certificate of origin card detailing the wall it came from, and a care guide.

The outer packaging is plain and discreet — ideal as a gift. You can add a personalised message at checkout at no extra charge.

Returns

Our returns policy.

Can I return my order?

Because each Urban Fragments piece is genuinely one-of-one, we are not able to accept returns based on a change of mind. We ask you to consider your purchase carefully before completing the order. The product page photographs and descriptions are provided in good faith to represent each piece as accurately as possible.

Damaged or incorrect items

If your piece arrives damaged or is not as described on the product page, please contact us at hello@urbanfragments.com within 7 days of receipt with your order number and photographs of the issue. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

How to start a return

Email hello@urbanfragments.com with the subject line Return request — [your order number]. Do not return items without contacting us first. Unauthorised returns will not be accepted.

Refund timeline

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed within 5 business days to your original payment method. You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been issued.
