Urban Fragments

Every city has
something worth
keeping.

Placeholder copy — client to provide

Urban Fragments is a jewellery collection that starts with a wall. We document painted surfaces across European cities — Basel, Berlin, Cologne, Ibiza, Leipzig, Lisbon, Luzern, Nuremberg, Vienna, Zürich — and reclaim fragments before they are demolished or painted over.

The fragment is set by hand into a piece of jewellery. The paint is original. Nothing is added.

Each piece is one-of-one. When a fragment is gone, it cannot be remade.

Urban Fragments is one of three collections made under Jasmine Hila Studios. Each collection begins with a material that would otherwise disappear — a wall, a surface, a found object — and transforms it into something worn and kept.

The studio works in small runs, without repeating itself. No piece is ever made twice. Urban Fragments is the first collection. Two more are in progress.

How it works

From wall
to wrist.

Every piece starts with the wall. We identify painted surfaces before demolition, carefully reclaim the fragment, set it by hand in our studio, and ship it with a full certificate of origin.

01Document — photograph and catalogue every wall
02Reclaim — remove the fragment, preserve the paint
03Set by hand — cut, finish, mount in sterling silver
04Certify — origin card ships with every piece
