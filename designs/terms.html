About Urban Fragments

Urban Fragments is a collection by Jasmine Hila Studios, a sole trader registered in Switzerland. These terms apply to all purchases made through urbanfragments.com. By completing a purchase, you agree to these terms.

One-of-one products

Every Urban Fragments piece is unique. Product photographs are taken under controlled conditions and are an accurate representation of each piece. Due to the nature of the materials, minor variations in colour, texture, or surface detail may occur between the photography environment and the piece as received. These are not defects — they are inherent to working with real reclaimed materials.

Pricing and payment

All prices are shown in euros (€) unless otherwise stated. Prices include VAT where applicable. Payment is processed securely through Shopify Payments. We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Your card is charged at the time of purchase.

Order confirmation

Once your order is placed, you will receive an automated confirmation email. This email is an acknowledgement of receipt, not acceptance of your order. We reserve the right to cancel any order at our discretion and will issue a full refund in such cases. You will receive a separate shipping confirmation email once your order has been dispatched.

Title and risk

Ownership of goods transfers to you once we receive full payment. Risk passes to you upon delivery to the address provided at checkout.

Customs and duties

For orders shipped outside Switzerland and the EU, customs duties and import taxes may apply. These charges are the responsibility of the buyer and are not included in the product price or shipping cost. We are unable to predict or control these charges.

Disputes

These terms are governed by Swiss law. Any disputes will be handled in accordance with Swiss jurisdiction. If you have a complaint, please contact us at hello@urbanfragments.com in the first instance and we will do our best to resolve the matter promptly.

Photography and content

Original wall art

Urban Fragments pieces incorporate fragments of original street art and graffiti. We document the original artist where known and include this information in the certificate of origin that ships with each piece. The act of reclaiming a fragment does not constitute endorsement by the original artist. We respect street art as a form of cultural expression and approach all reclamations with care.

Privacy policy

How we use your data.

What we collect

When you place an order, we collect the information necessary to fulfil it: name, delivery address, email address, and payment details. Payment details are processed by Shopify Payments and are never stored on our servers. If you sign up to our newsletter, we store your email address for that purpose only.

How we use your data

We use your data to: process and ship your order, communicate with you about your order, send you the newsletter if you have opted in, and improve the website experience. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third parties

We use the following services which may process your data: Shopify (e-commerce platform and payments), Klaviyo (email marketing, if you opt in), Google Analytics (anonymised website analytics). Each of these services operates under its own privacy policy.

Your rights

Under GDPR and applicable Swiss data protection law, you have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@urbanfragments.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Data retention

We retain order data for 7 years as required by Swiss accounting law. Newsletter subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any newsletter email.

Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, contact us at hello@urbanfragments.com. Our registered address is available on request.

Cookies

Cookie policy.

What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember information about your visit — for example, the contents of your bag or your preferred language.

Cookies we use

Essential cookies are required for the shop to function — they maintain your session, remember your bag, and process checkout. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) collect anonymous data about how visitors use the site — which pages are viewed, how long visitors stay, and where they come from. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individuals.

Marketing cookies may be set if you interact with social media features on this site (Instagram embeds, etc). These are controlled by the relevant platform.

Managing cookies

You can disable non-essential cookies via your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect some aspects of the shopping experience.
