About the pieces
Each piece is made from a real fragment of a graffiti-painted wall. We cut sections from painted concrete, brick, or metal surfaces before they are demolished or repainted. The fragment is then set into sterling silver (some pieces are gold-plated). The paint you see is exactly as it appeared on the wall.
Yes. Every piece is genuinely one-of-one. Because each comes from a specific section of a specific wall, no two fragments are identical. Once a piece is sold, it cannot be recreated.
Where possible, yes. We document each wall before reclaiming and try to identify and credit the original artist. This information is included in the certificate of origin that ships with each piece. In some cases, the artist is unknown.
The fragments are set securely in sterling silver and designed to be worn daily. The paint surface is naturally robust but should be kept away from harsh chemicals, perfumes, and prolonged exposure to water. See our care guide for details.
Ordering & payment
Available pieces are clearly marked on the product page. Sold pieces remain visible in the archive as a record, marked "Sold — one of a kind." We update the shop in real time.
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) as well as PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay, all processed securely through Shopify.
We don't currently offer reservations. Pieces are sold on a first-come, first-served basis. If you're interested in a specific piece, we recommend purchasing it as soon as you see it — they do not come back.
Yes. All orders ship in our standard packaging, which is gift-ready. You can add a personalised message at checkout at no extra charge.
Shipping
We ship worldwide. See our Shipping & Returns page for rates and delivery times by region.
Europe: 3–7 business days. International: 7–14 business days. Express options are available at checkout. All orders are dispatched within 2 business days of payment confirmation.
Each piece ships in a small branded box with tissue paper, the certificate of origin card, and a care guide. The outer packaging is plain and discreet.
Returns & care
Because each piece is one-of-one, we are not able to accept returns on the basis of changed preference. However, if a piece arrives damaged or is not as described, please contact us within 7 days and we will resolve it. See our full returns policy.
Keep away from water, perfume, and harsh chemicals. Clean gently with a soft dry cloth. Store separately to avoid scratching. The silver setting can be polished with a standard silver cloth if needed.
