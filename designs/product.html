This U|F vie004 is a unique piece from the URBAN FRAGMENTS collection, handcrafted from real graffiti collected in Vienna. This fragment captures a raw slice of urban expression transformed into wearable art.

Its vivid layering tells a visual story of the street it came from. Lightweight and expressive, it stands as both an accessory and a conversation piece. As each piece is made from real graffiti, no two are ever the same. You're holding a literal fragment of the city.

We ship worldwide. Europe: 3–7 business days. International: 7–14 days. Free shipping on orders over €200 within Europe.

As this is a one-of-one piece, we cannot accept returns based on change of mind. See our full returns policy.

Keep away from water, perfume, and harsh chemicals. Clean gently with a soft dry cloth. Store separately to avoid scratching. The silver setting can be polished with a standard silver cloth.

Where it came from

Reclaimed
from Vienna.

This piece was cut from a painted wall in Vienna's 7th district before demolition in 2023. The turquoise layers were part of a larger mural — this fragment carries the original paint exactly as it appeared on the wall. Nothing has been added.
